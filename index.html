Hi there! I’m Anahita Bolourani (Ana), a Ph.D. candidate at UCLA who is passionate about artificial intelligence—especially diffusion models and large language models. My research blends advanced statistical theory with state‑of‑the‑art machine‑learning tools such as PyTorch and TensorFlow to push the limits of how we model, generate, and understand complex data. From image synthesis to seismic analysis and emotion‑aware systems, I explore ways generative models can drive real‑world impact.

Welcome to my GitHub page, where you’ll find my latest projects, publications, and occasional reflections on the future of AI.
